Talents in Spotlight
This month, we bring you two stunning performers lighting up the live music scene with their unique takes on Qawwali and Sufi fusion—Bismil and Sagar Wali Qawwali. Often flying under the radar, these artists have steadily built a loyal following with their mesmerizing stage presence and soul-stirring renditions.
Bismil
A Musical Band best known for eclectic performances being a mix of shayaris, qawwalis, and classic tunes
Bismil is a dynamic multi-genre Hindi band, led by the soulful vocalist Asif Khan. Known for its unique blend of Sufi, Bollywood, and retro hits, Bismil captivates audiences with a perfect mix of shayaris, qawwalis, and classic tunes. Inspired by legends like Ustad Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan and AR Rahman, the band creates a mesmerizing musical journey. With each live performance, Bismil delivers an unforgettable experience that seamlessly fuses class with mass appeal, making everyone fall in love with their sound.

Sagar Wali Qawwali
A Musical Maestro enticing audiences with his modern take on traditional Qawwali music
Sagar Bhatia, the voice behind the moniker 'Sagar Wali Qawwali' is a musical maestro redefining Qawwali for the modern era. From his rise on India’s Rawstar to rocking over 2000 stages worldwide, Sagar Bhatia’s soulful voice weaves tradition with contemporary flair. His live shows are a vibrant blend of high-energy qawwalis with contemporary rhythms that have garnered him massive fan followings both in India and abroad.

Peprico Band

Raaga Fusion

Zero Trust Meets AI: ZScaler's Zenith Live '24 Sets New Standards in Cloud Security

Hormuzd Khambatta Dance Company

Virender Sehwag

KK Live Forever

ZScaler’s Zenith Live '24 was a premier event designed for C-level executives and industry leaders, bringing together around 1,200 attendees to explore the intersection of AI and Zero Trust security. The event kicked off with a vibrant performance by the Hormuzd Khambatta Dance Company, featuring 30 dancers in an exhilarating flash mob that transitioned into an upbeat dance sequence, setting a high-energy tone for the day. Virender Sehwag, the Indian cricketer renowned for his explosive and fearless batting, inspired attendees with a riveting session on leadership and resilience. The event concluded with a heartfelt tribute to the late Bollywood singer KK, by the band KK Live Forever, a band composed of musicians who used to perform alongside the legendary singer himself. The lead vocalist, with an uncanny resemblance to KK's signature voice and style, transported the audience back to the legendary singer's iconic era. The tribute celebrated KK's enduring legacy with his timeless hits, leaving the crowd nostalgic.

SAP’s Flagship Event in Delhi

Radhika Gupta

Leander Paes

In a grand celebration of India's journey from "Incredible to Inevitable," SAP NOW New Delhi brought together over 600 leaders for an immersive experience. The event highlighted the pivotal role of technology in shaping the nation’s future, with engaging keynotes and immersive AI demonstrations. Taking the spotlight was Radhika Gupta, MD and CEO of Edelweiss Asset Management, known for her trailblazing leadership and advocacy for women in finance, who shared powerful insights during a fireside chat on breaking the glass ceiling in business. Infusing further inspiration was tennis icon Leander Paes, celebrated for his 18 Grand Slam titles and known as one of the greatest doubles players in history. He delivered an inspiring session on building high-performance teams, drawing from his legendary sports career. The entertainment quotient was brought in by musical maestro Hariharan, revered for his soulful voice and iconic songs like "Tu Hi Re," captivated the audience with a live performance, while Carnatic 2.0 delivered a mesmerizing fusion act that blended tradition and modernity.
